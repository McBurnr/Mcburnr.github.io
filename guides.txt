<p>The battle system in P5X revolves around exploiting enemy weaknesses to gain "1 More" action. This is the core loop of combat.</p>
<ul>
    <li><b>Physical & Gun:</b> Standard damage types.</li>
    <li><b>Elemental Skills:</b> Fire, Ice, Electric, Wind, Psy, Nuke, Bless, and Curse.</li>
</ul>

[GUIDE]
TITLE: Gacha Banners Explained
CATEGORY: Gacha
AUTHOR: Tenacity
AUTHOR_IMAGE: https://cdn.imgchest.com/files/yd5ce5zlmd4.png
CONTENT:
<p>The gacha system has two primary banner types for characters: <b>Fated Bond</b> and <b>Exclusive Bond</b>.</p>
<p><b>Fated Bond:</b> This is an 80-pity banner with a 50/50 chance to get the featured character. If you lose the 50/50, your next 5-star is guaranteed to be the featured one.</p>
