Terraria 1.4.5

Comprehensive changelog featuring Dead Cells content, new whips, seeds, and QoL.

Dead Cells & Others

New
  • The Beheaded: Vanity outfit of the protagonist.
  • Weapons:
    • The Flint (Melee)
    • The Killing Deck (Magic/Ranged)
    • Mushroom Staff (Summons Mushroom Boi)
  • Furniture/Items: Hanging Flasks, Barnacle Sentry, The Swarm, Ram Rune, Wings of the Crow, Health Fountain.
  • Palworld: Summonable "Digtoise" minion/pet that mines blocks.

Whips & Summoner

Expansion
Massive expansion to Summoner progression:
  • Moon Lord Whip
  • Stardust Whip
  • Soulscourge (Corruption)
  • Vasculash (Crimson)
  • Duke Fishron Whip
  • Plantera Whip
  • White Whip
  • Slime Whip (Early game)
New Modifiers: Eager, Focused, Loyal, Worthy, Rabid, Ballistic, Fabled for summon weapons.

Arsenal Additions

  • Axerang: Axe + Boomerang hybrid. Chops trees when thrown.
  • Slime Spear: Early game gel weapon.
  • Explosives: Freeze Bomb (creates ice), Hardmode Ore Bomb.
  • Mud Balls: Throwable, places mud blocks.
  • Acorn Slingshot: Shoots acorns that plant saplings on impact.
  • Magic String: Accessory that detaches Yoyos to deal damage freely.

Building & Decor

Creative
New Furniture Sets:

Hallow, Aetherium, Fallen Star, Feywood, Corruption, Crimson, Gothic, Stone, and Modern.

  • Functional: Hoppers (store dropped items), Music Blocks (play notes), CRT Television (visual filters), "Mitey-Titey" (places stalactites).
  • Placeable Objects: Demon Altars, Fallen Logs, Life Fruit, Shadow Orbs, Cannonballs.
  • Blocks: Fallen Star Bricks/Walls, Cloud Platforms, Unsafe Living Wood Walls.

Boulders & Traps

  • Reverse Gravity Boulders (fall up).
  • Boulder Rain: New weather event.
  • Rainbow Boulder: Bounces between ores/chests.
  • Poo Boulder & Moon Lord Boulders.

World Generation

  • Seed Mixing: New menu allows combining secret seeds (e.g., GetFixedBoi + Celebrationmk10).
  • Skyblock Seed: Spawns player on a floating island with limited resources.
  • Vampire Features: "Vampire Seed" mechanics where player burns in sun.
  • Meteor Showers: New event causing meteorite to fall.
  • Dungeon: Two new entrance structure variations.

QoL & Systems

Major
  • • Shimmer Water Gun: Ranged NPC transmutation.
  • • Smart Cursor: Improved logic for platform placement.
  • • Stacking: Weapons/Tools/Voodoo Dolls now stack (cannot reforge stack).
  • • Banners: No longer drop; claimed via menu.
  • • NPCs: Housing hints added. Houses no longer strictly require a solid block to stand on.
  • • Loadouts: Copy/Paste enabled.
  • • Mobile: Advanced touch control editor with multi-touch toggle.

Mounts & Pets

  • Transformations: Wolf, Bat, Rat, Fairy, Velociraptor (dyeable).
  • Mounts: Roller Skates (grind rails), Kites on sticks.
  • Pets: Axe Fairy (Cenx avatar), Pufferfish.
  • Accessories: Stress Ball (auto-attack), Audio changers (Goat, Zombie, Turkey, Chicken, Arcade).

Visuals & Audio

  • • Resprites: Moon Lord, Imp Staff, Slime Staff, Trophies, Banners, Demon Altars.
  • • New Music: 10+ new tracks including Boss themes (Twins, EoW, Skeletron).
  • • Ambience: Fireflies/insects in grass, Film Noir shader.
  • • Paintings: New community art + r/place 2023 canvas.
  • • Character Creation: Voice pitch slider, new voice option.
