A Final Message

It was really fun working on these websites! What started as a small website, became a passion project. However, there's better websites, and I couldn't keep up with maintaining the website. The host broke the images, and I'm not the best at website creation. But I think I did pretty alright. What do you think? The websites will remain up, just under different names. If you want to see the units, then visit: https://mcburnr.github.io/p5xunitsarchive.html If you want to see the main page, then visit: https://mcburnr.github.io/p5xwikiarchive.html If you want up to date guides and an active community, why not join the P5X Worldwide Discord?: https://discord.gg/P5XWorldWide Or if you want to join the server I'm usually on, we're over at: https://discord.gg/Ch2rTqmta8 During times the website wasn't being worked on, well....I was working on this. It's unfortunate, but I don't have a lot of time for it anymore. Thankfully, there's others who are way more passionate and working hard on their own websites.
